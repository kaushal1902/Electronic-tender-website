Frequently Asked Questions

To submit a tender application, first register and verify your account. Then browse available tenders, select one you're interested in, and click "Apply". Fill out the application form with your proposal, price quote, and completion time.

Tender winners are selected based on a scoring system that evaluates price quotes (50%) and completion time (50%). The system automatically calculates scores for approved applications, and administrators make the final selection.

You can track your application status in the "My Applications" section. Status updates will be sent to your registered email address. Possible statuses include pending, approved, and rejected.
